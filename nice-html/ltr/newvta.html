Hydroponics
Hydroponics is a cutting-edge agricultural technique that transforms the way plants are cultivated by eliminating the need for soil. Instead, plants are grown in nutrient-rich water solutions, providing essential minerals directly to their roots. This method offers numerous advantages over traditional soil-based farming, with plants growing faster and more efficiently due to the direct delivery of water and oxygen.
